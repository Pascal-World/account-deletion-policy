Account Deletion Policy

How to Delete Your Account

If you would like to delete your account and remove your associated data from St Paul GRA Nsk, follow the steps below:

Steps to Delete Your Account

  • Open the St Paul GRA Nsk app on your device.
  • Navigate to Account Tab and Scroll down to Delete Account Button.
  • Tap on Delete My Account.
  • Confirm your request by following the on-screen instructions.
  • Your account and data will be permanently deleted within 10 days.

If you are unable to delete your account through the app, you may request deletion by contacting us at stpaulgransukka@gmail.com with the subject line Account Deletion Request.

What Data is Deleted?

When you request account deletion, the following data will be removed from our servers:

  • Personal information (e.g., name, email address, image).

What Data is Retained?

No data is retained. Once your account is deleted, all associated data is permanently removed from our servers.

How Long Does the Process Take?

Once your deletion request is submitted, your data will be permanently deleted within 10 days. If you need assistance or have concerns, please contact us at stpaulgransukka@gmail.com.
